Palikkapeli (Block Game)

University Touch Table Processing Java

Developed: 2012

Platforms: Multi-touch table

Team size: 3

Role: Designer & Developer

An Experimental User Interfaces course project made together with Kenta Kusumoto and Antti Seppä. Our goal was to create a game that is both cooperative and competetive. Two teams compete by creating simple shapes and "throwing" them to the opposing team's side. The catch is that to create these shapes, often more then two hands are needed. The game is built with Processing.

Master's Thesis

University Research R Processing

University Aalto University

Completed: 2013

Thesis project examining the theoretical and practical process of exploratory information visualization. The project consisted of research into the process of exploratory visualization as well as the iterative development of tailored visualization tools for Aalto University's Institutional Relationships unit.

The resulting thesis is titled Exploratory Visualization of Inter-Organizational Networks. The visualization tools were built with Processing and additional graphs for the thesis were made in R.
