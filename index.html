Last.fm Album Charter

Find albums you should listen to

Do you ever miss the news that an artist you like has released a new album? Or perhaps you've forgotten an older one. This chart will show your top artists and highlight their albums according to how much you have listened to them.

About

Albums are filtered by name to avoid duplicates and special editions, so it's possible some albums are missing.

Built with JavaScript last.fm API and D3.js.
